## Features

- **Dual Streak System**: Track both daily (max 3 uploads per day) and weekly (max 21 uploads per week) streaks
- **Visual Calendars**: Monthly heat map for daily progress and yearly view for weekly consistency
- **Profile Customization**: Upload a profile picture and set your username
- **Achievement Titles**: Unlock 38+ titles across different tiers (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythic) as you build your streaks
- **Milestone Notifications**: Get celebratory alerts when you hit major milestones (7 days, 30 days, 100 days, etc.)
- **Art Gallery**: Browse and view all your uploaded artwork in a beautiful grid layout
- **Smart Streak Logic**: Automatically resets daily counts at midnight and weekly counts on Sundays
- **Local Storage**: All your data is saved locally on your deviceâ€”no account needed

## How to Use

1. **Set Your Name**: Enter your artist name in the profile section
2. **Upload Your Art**: Click "Choose Image" to upload your daily artwork (limit: 3 per day)
3. **Watch Your Streaks Grow**: See your daily and weekly streaks increase with each upload
4. **Unlock Titles**: As your streaks grow, new titles unlock that you can display on your profile
5. **Check Your Progress**: View the calendar heat maps to visualize your consistency
6. **Browse Your Gallery**: Click any image to view it in fullscreen
